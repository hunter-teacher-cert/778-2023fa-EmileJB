You discover the lost party. It seems that they've met their end by petrification. You almost stop to mourn for a bit until you realize that maybe it isn't their end. You ask your animal companion to fetch your cleric associate, which means you need to stick around in the dungeon awhile and stand guard. Your companion eventually returns with a cleric and blesses the party with a Greater Restoration spell returning them to normal. You feel good having found and saved them.

Despite previous encounters, you decide to examine the three statues for clues and it sure is a good thing you did! Those statues appear to be the missing party you're looking for; a dwarf warrior, a human wizard, and an elven archer. You decide to start thinking about how this could've happened. A basilisk maybe, but this dungeon design seems too deliberate for that. An evil spellcaster comes to mind, but you're not sure about that. You consider a gorgon as the halfway point between the two ideas.
