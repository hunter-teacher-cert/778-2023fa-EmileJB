The clarity slider affects the details of the image. Decreasing clarity softens the image while increasing clarity sharpens the image. Both the vibrance slider and saturation slider affect the color of the image, although they work in different ways. Vibrance is more selective and affects the bright colors already in the image while saturation affects the entire image. Decreasing dullens the image and increasing brightens colors for both sliders, but because of how they work saturation seems to be more intense. The shadows slider affects the darkness of the image, but while still preserving detail. Increasing shadows increases darkness while decreasing shadows decreases darkness. The blacks slider functions in a similar way however it does not intend to preserve details, so it has a much greater affect than the shadows slider.
